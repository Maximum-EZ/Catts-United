Training Guide

This is only for the host and co-host, but you may look at it to get more info before joining a training session. LRC = Low Rank Cats.
Host: Greetings, everyone! Welcome to today’s training session! My name is (username), and I am your host.
Co-Host: Hello, everyone! My name is (username), and I am your co-host for today’s training session. I wish you the best of luck.
Co-Host: Before we commence, I ask everyone to remain silent at all times. We will be giving you a list of expectations and rules.
Host: This lasts a few minutes, so we request that you give us your full attention, as we are teaching you vital information for your success.
Co-Host: Please note that speaking or distracting others is prohibited during this time, and you will be removed from the server if caught doing so.
Host: Thank you for paying attention during our presentation. You will now receive further instructions about this training.
Host: LRCs, please remain on the red lines. You will be teleported to the training game soon.
